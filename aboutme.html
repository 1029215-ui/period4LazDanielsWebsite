About Me

Hello! I'm Daniel, and this is my website where I share my interests and projects.

It began with the idea of wanting to learn what I could create when working with technology

This is my secodn year at El Camino High School and I first became fascinated by how people used software to create visual art and crafts, which sparked my curiosity and led me to explore further. As I delved into 3D design and gained some hands-on experience, I realized how much I enjoyed working with digital tools and experimenting with creative possibilities. This naturally led me to explore Graphic Design and now Web Design.
